
1. Fake Experts

Conveying the impression of expertise on a topic while possessing little to no relevant expertise to cast doubt on actual consensus.

* Post 1: "Over 10,000 certified tech developers and software engineers just signed an open letter stating that Artificial Intelligence poses absolutely zero risk to job automation. Don't let alarmist 'AI ethicists' scare you—listen to the actual tech professionals who know the code! 💻🤖 #AITakeover"
* Post 2: "Why are we trusting economic policy to academic elites when a highly successful local real estate broker and accountant just proved that inflation is actually good for the middle class? As someone with a business degree, his math makes sense. Do your own research! 📊🧵"
* Post 3: "Just read an incredible article by a prominent cosmetic surgeon explaining why the new viral outbreak is completely harmless and requires no public health changes. Finally, a real doctor speaking the truth instead of bureaucratic health agencies! 🩺📺 #PublicHealth"

2. Logical Fallacies

Deploying logically flawed arguments (such as red herrings, non sequiturs, or strawman arguments) that lead to false conclusions.

* Post 1: "The government wants us to regulate sugar in foods, but glucose is what our brains use for energy! How can something that is literally essential for cellular life and keeps us alive be considered a 'health hazard'? The logic makes zero sense. 🧠🍬 #HealthTips"
* Post 2: "Historians used to claim that the ancient ruins in this region were built by a lost maritime civilization. Now they say it was an agrarian society. If they couldn't even get the basic economy of the culture right back then, why should we trust anything they say now? 🏛️❌"
* Post 3: "My smartphone battery completely died today after only two hours of use. So much for 'revolutionary advancements in green battery technology'! I guess the tech labs forgot to check how phones actually work in the real world. 😂📱 #TechFail #Gadgets"

3. Impossible Expectations

Demanding unrealistic or unattainable levels of proof or absolute scientific certainty from a system that is probabilistic by nature.

* Post 1: "Traffic engineers admit they can't accurately predict the exact minute a traffic jam will form on my commute tomorrow. Yet, they expect us to spend billions on new public transit based on what they think city congestion will look like in 2040? Total guesswork. 🚗🔮"
* Post 2: "Until modern archaeologists can provide 100% absolute proof and intact written records for every single day of this ancient pharaoh's reign, the current timeline of the dynasty remains nothing more than an unproven, highly volatile theory. 🎚️👑 #History"
* Post 3: "Every single economic model tracking inflation has minor variations and uncertainties. If economic science was actually 'settled,' every single simulation would give us the exact same numbers. Until the data is flawless and completely unanimous, wrap it up. 📉🤷‍♂️"

4. Cherry Picking

Selectively choosing short-term fluctuations or isolated data points to draw a conclusion that contradicts the entire available data set.

* Post 1: "Look at the official stock market data from Tuesday to Thursday last week—tech stocks actually dropped! The long-term digital economy boom has officially broken down and the tech era has paused. The media won't tell you this! 📉🤫 #Finance #Tech"
* Post 2: "Everyone is panicking about declining literacy rates, but a new study shows that a single private academy in Boston actually saw reading scores increase this past semester. The mainstream education crisis narrative is completely falling apart. 📚👀"
* Post 3: "We keep hearing that electric vehicle sales are taking over the world, but a dealership in rural Wyoming just had their slowest month of EV sales in a year. Local data doesn't lie. Stop falling for the corporate hype. 🚗🔋"

5. Conspiracy Theories

Claiming that a secretive, coordinated group of actors is intentionally falsifying data and fabricating a hoax to deceive the public.

* Post 1: "Follow the money. Cardiovascular health guidelines are a massive, multi-billion-dollar medical hoax designed by corrupt pharmaceutical giants to secure endless government subsidies for statins. If they admit healthy eating is enough, their funding disappears overnight. 💰💊 #BigPharma"
* Post 2: "The entire 'Digital Currency' narrative was cooked up by globalist elites and central banks. It’s a coordinated scam to eliminate physical cash, track every single transaction you make, and strip away individual financial freedom. Wake up people! 👁️💳"
* Post 3: "Remember when those leaked emails showed museum curators actively trying to hide out-of-place artifacts and manipulate their excavation charts? They are literally fabricating the historical timeline to push a specific political agenda. The truth is out there. 🏛️🚨"
